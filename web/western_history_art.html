Western History of Painting

Website

Logo, Visual Design, UI design, Interaction Design

This site was designed and developed for my thesis project in 2012. The main idea of the site is to present to the users all the information that is related to western painting in a nice and friendly environment.

Logo and colour palette

Based on the fact that it's a site about the history of western painting, it was decided to have a classical character but still stay modern. The logo was designed to be classy while avoiding being heavy. Additionally, the tint of white and a shade of grey has been used to add up on the classical feeling. For the design of the site, a palette with more tones of grey has been used as well as a green tint.

Logo colour palette

website colour pallete

Visual design

Big images of the paintings have been used in the whole site, as they are the main interest, adding colour to the colourless pages. No colours have been used for the pages' elements, except the colour of the links, but rather tones of grey and white. The whole idea was to have a minimal design which focuses on the colours and shapes of the paintings instead.

UI and Interaction design

In the painter's page, the visitor can see the biography of the painter or the information of his paintings or either hide them to see the details of the painting which is represented on the background.
