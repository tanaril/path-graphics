Markant

Website

Visual Design, interaction Design

This website was redesigned in 2013 and it is still under construction, for the Markant women's orginization of Zwijnaarde. Other than a new fresh design, it was requested a better functionality of their site and a reconstruction of its information. The main purpose of the site is the the members to be informed of the organization's upcoming events.

Website colour palette

Additional to the logo's dominant red, two tones of grey have been used, one lighter and one darker, for highlighting the organization's colour while it stays simple.

Visual design

A big calendar was designed for the upcoming events which describes the events in detail.
