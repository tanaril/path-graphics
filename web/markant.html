Website

Visual Design, interaction Design

This website was created in 2013 and it is still under constraction, for the women's orginization of Zwijnaarde. They wanted a fresh design as well as better functionality of their site. The information of this site is not that much and its main focus is on the events they orginize for their members.

Website colour palette

Additionally to the dominant logo's red, two tones of grey have been used, one lighter and one darker, so the red and thus the branding of the page will pop out.

Visual design

As the site's main purpose was the organized events, a big calendar was created in which the members can see the upcoming events in detail.
