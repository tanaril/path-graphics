The Love Fairy

Website

Logo, Visual and Graphic Design, UI Design, E-mail Design

The Love Fairy is an idea of an acquaintance of mine which is planned to run in all the digital forms. For the beginning though, it was decided to run on the web only, with the thought of a future mobile app. The idea is similar to a dating site, but with the big difference of knowing the person who gets your request. It was designed in 2013, its currently online but still under development.

Logo and colour palette

The logo was designed to be playful. The font that has been used is rounded and light, while some tones of grey have been used to add on the lightness and make the fairy graphic to pop out. For the site, shades of grey have been used additionally to the cherry and blue colours which represent the genders.

Visual and graphic design

The aim was to make the site neutral, neither boyish or girlish, while it remains cheerfulness.

UI design

The user interface was designed to be clear, with very good functionality, making it easy for the users to avoid or recover from errors. It was also designed with mobile and tablet users in mind.
