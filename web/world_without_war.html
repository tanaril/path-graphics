World Without Wars and Violence

Website

Visual Design, UI Design, information architecture

This website was designed in 2013, for the greek hub of World without Wars and Violence organization. They wanted a fresh redesign and a new information architecture approach, due to the mass information they provide in their current site, which can't handle anymore. This site is still under construction, but here is a sense of how it will look like.

Website colour palette

Due to the informative character of the site and the minimal approach, I have decided to use only some tints of grey, additionally to the basic colour of the organization logo.

Visual design

It was decided to have a flat and minimal approach for the design, which will rather focus on the information. In their home page they wanted all the new articles to be shown, as well as a calendar which will show the upcoming events for their members or other people who are interested in.
