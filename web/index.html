About

Hello, my name is Themis Papaioannou and I'm currently living in Belgium. I have worked in the web and design industry for three years, cooparating with big companies, as well as working as a freelancer. I'm a big fan of simplicity and minimalism, I love solving problems for the digital environment and creating greater user experiences, with pixel perfect attention to detail. I am fascinated with innovative technologies and it's my aim to be a part of this technology and informatics revolution.

I am planning on moving to London in the near future and looking for a full time position in the area. Please, feel free to contact me if you have any job inquiries, questions or just want to say Hi!
