Website

Visual and graphic Design, interaction Design

This site was created in 2012 for the children's camp in Zwijnaarde. The main purpose of this site was the presentation of the details of the upcoming events, for the children and their parents, as well as the information of each youth group's role and of the leaders. Due to the slow working of the organization on the texts and the material of the pages, it's currently offline.

Website colour palette

Two shades of grey has been selected for coming in constrast with the white of the logo, as well as a tint of green that adds some life but also points out the enviromental character of the organization's activities.

Visual and Graphic design

The site was designed not only with the children in mind but also the adult group. It has minimal approach, but it keeps its playfulness with the selected fonts, the shapes and the illustrations.
