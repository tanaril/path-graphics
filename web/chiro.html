Website

Visual and graphic Design, interaction Design

This site was created in 2012 for the children's camp in Zwijnaarde. The main purpose of this site was for the parents and children to see the upcoming events of the camp as well as the information of the leaders of the groups. It's currently offline due to the fact that the organization hasn't collected the texts of the pages.

Website colour palette

Two shades of grey has been used for coming in constrast with the white of the logo, as well as a tint of green that points out the enviromental activities and character of the camp.

Visual and Graphic design

The site was created with minimal approach, but at the same time it keeps its playfulness with the selection of the fonts as well as the graphics that has been designed.
